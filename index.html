About Florida

Florida joined the United States as the 27th state in 1845 and while not the largest state in the U.S., Florida has the 3rd largest population at over 23 million. A state of many sights and attractions, Florida sits at the Southeastern edge of the United States.

The 'Sunshine State' sees an average of over 100 million visitors every year and is best known for its world-famous attractions like Disney World, Miami Beach, and the Everglades National Park.
